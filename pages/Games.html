3D Ray Caster made in scratch

Use [WASD] to move and [SPACE] to spring. [ARROW KEYS] turn. This game takes a map I drew and depending on the distance from the player sprite to the wall, draws a line on the screen corosponding to the closest point and smaller lines marking further points on the walls. This technique is called Raycasting and is used to fake 3D on platforms or hardware not strong enough to run proper 3D.
